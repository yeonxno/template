The Atelier

Where restraint is a craft

Founded in Copenhagen in 1980. Eleven makers, one workshop, forty commissions a year. Nothing leaves the bench unsigned.

Design Philosophy

Subtract until it holds

Every LUMEN // 80 piece begins as an over-drawn sketch. The work of the studio is removal: taking away line after line until only the structure that carries weight, light and use remains.

We do not follow seasons. A chair drawn in 1984 still sits in the current catalogue because nothing about sitting has changed.

Material Provenance

Every plank has an address

  • Oak & AshCertified forests in Jämtland, Sweden. Felled in winter, milled within 40 km, air-dried on site for three years.
  • WoolTwo family-run Gotland flocks. Sheared, sorted and spun on the island; never bleached, never dyed.
  • Basalt & TravertineQuarried in the Eifel region and Tivoli. Offcuts return to the quarry for aggregate, not landfill.
  • BrassMinimum 85% recycled content, cast in a fourth-generation Copenhagen foundry twelve minutes from our bench.
Sustainable Woodcraft

Joinery instead of hardware

Our frames are cut with wedged tenons and dovetails, not screws and brackets. A piece that is joined can be re-joined; a piece that is glued and bolted usually cannot.

Every commission includes a lifetime refinishing service. We would rather restore a thirty-year-old table than sell a new one to the same address.
